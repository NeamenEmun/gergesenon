About Gergesenon Mental Rehabilitation Center

For decades, we have provided compassionate mental health care and rehabilitation across Ethiopia. Discover our mission, meet our founder, and see the lives we've transformed.

Our Impact

Transforming lives through compassionate mental health care and community rehabilitation.

Gergesenon Mental Rehabilitation Center provides shelter, treatment, and long-term rehabilitation for individuals with severe mental health challenges in Addis Ababa. Over the past decade, we have supported 262 people, with 85 successfully recovering and reintegrating into their communities.

Recognized for community-driven approaches, our programs combine professional expertise with culturally-sensitive care, offering hope and dignity to those in need.

Meles Gergesenon

Founder & Director

Driven by compassion and a vision for accessible mental health care, Meles established Gergesenon Mental Rehabilitation Center to provide shelter, treatment, and dignity for those facing severe mental challenges. His leadership emphasizes culturally-sensitive care and community integration.
